New Balance Case Study

MGT 302: Intro to Supply Chain Management - Spring 2012

For this case study, My group was given information on New Balance from 2006 and we were asked to analyze the current business model and propose changes that would improve the company and provide reasons. We also presented to several real world executives from various firms who them ranked each group. My group placed first.
